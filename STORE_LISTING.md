# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name

Deep Flow

## Short Description

A minimal focus timer for deep work. Set a task, start the timer, and review your session history — all in your browser's side panel.

## Detailed Description

Deep Flow is a clean, distraction-free focus timer that lives in your browser's side panel.

Set a task, choose a duration, and enter deep work mode — without leaving your browser.

KEY FEATURES

• Focus Timer — Choose from 15, 30, 45, or 60 minute sessions.
• Ambient Sound — Optional background audio to help you stay in the zone. Mute or unmute anytime.
• Pause & Resume — Step away when needed and pick up right where you left off.
• Early Completion — Finish ahead of schedule and still save your progress.
• Session History — Review past sessions with total focus time tracked automatically.
• Reflection Notes — Add a short note after each session to capture what you accomplished.
• Edit & Delete — Update task names, notes, or remove sessions from your history.
• Dark / Light / System Theme — Matches your preference or follows your OS setting.
• Completion Alerts — Get a notification and sound when your timer ends.

PRIVACY

Deep Flow stores everything locally on your device. No accounts, no sign-ups, no data sent to any server. Your session history and settings stay in your browser and can be cleared at any time.

All source code is open and auditable. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
